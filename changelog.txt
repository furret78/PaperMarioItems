Paper Mario Items 0.1.6 -- Update notes
- Added Mistake
- Added rudimentary cooking UI:
  + Any cooking that does not follow a pre-defined recipe will return a Mistake (note: not every recipe has been added)
  + Accessing the cooking menu requires a Cookbook present in the player's inventory
  + Certain crafting recipes may not exist in the cooking system
- Removed all cooking-related crafting recipes that had been added in 0.1.5. Cooking will be done via a Cooking Pot instead
- Reduced the drop rate of Inn Coupons
