Paper Mario Items 0.7.1 -- Update notes
- Added Castle Bleck Brick, Castle Bleck Wall, Castle Bleck Door
- Life Mushrooms now heal 50 health on consumption at 0 health. Additionally, invincibility timeframe (1 minute => 30 seconds) and health generation timeframe (2 minutes => 1 minute) given when revived has been nerfed.
- Revival via Life Mushrooms now properly prevent On Fire from applying when revived in lava.
